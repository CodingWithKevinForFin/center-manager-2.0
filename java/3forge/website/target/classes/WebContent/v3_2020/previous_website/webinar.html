When it comes to capital markets technology, the biggest opportunity for firms today is to rethink their approach to how they build, buy, and bind their complicated tech and app stacks in a time of exploding data and cost pressures.

In this webinar, John Lowrey, former Global Head of Electronic Markets (Equities) at Citi, discusses key next-gen IT strategies from colocation, cloud, low-code to microservices.

The talk also provides practical guidance on how to adapt to these new trends with first-hand insights from Robert Cooke, CTO at 3forge, a performant and flexible low-code platform used by the world's leading banks across the world.

The Path to Low Code

The trading ecosystem has grown up in distinct and difficult to breakdown silos across asset classes. It is often bespoke, point focused, and massively siloed. Infrastructure, f/o, back/middle/risk compliance have evolved along separate lines and robust interoperability is a massive tax from a risk and cost perspective.

In this report, Celent - a leading research and advisory firm specializing in Financial Services - explores the whys of this transition and its implications in greater detail. It also looks at the modern functional app stack.
