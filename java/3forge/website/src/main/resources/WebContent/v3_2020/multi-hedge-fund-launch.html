Empowering Hedge Fund Success With 3forge

About the company

This is the story of Jain Global, the largest hedge fund launch since 2018, which leveraged 3forge's advanced platform to redefine operational efficiency and scalability. From overcoming launch challenges to managing intricate operations across seven diverse business lines, this case study reveals how 3Forge enabled Jain Global to achieve unparalleled success.

Challenge

Launching a hedge fund on a scale requires meticulous planning and execution. For Jain Global, the stakes were even higher due to its size and scope. The firm's IT team collaborated closely with 3forge to create a data infrastructure capable of handling unprecedented volumes of real-time market data.

Solution

Jain Global's transition from traditional end-of-day risk assessments to real-time risk monitoring marked a significant milestone. By leveraging 3forge's powerful aggregation engine, the firm achieved intraday visibility across all seven business lines. This allowed executives and PMs to respond immediately to market fluctuations, improving decision making and operational efficiency.

Benefits

Jain Global's journey offers valuable insights for hedge funds looking to scale.

  • Strategic Technology Adoption: Choosing a flexible, scalable platform like 3forge can transform operational challenges into competitive advantages.
  • Cost Efficiency: Streamlining operations and eliminating manual processes reduce costs while enabling rapid growth.
